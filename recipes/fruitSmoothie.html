Fruit Smoothie

This is a fresh and easy/quick to make fruit smoothie. It is very nutrious and healthy for the anyone to try and is in needed for a filling yet not very calorie heavy snack. It is also quite cheap to make, which also why people would favor it.

Ingredients

  • 1 kiwi
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup hulled strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice

Steps

  1. Combine kiwi, banana, strawberries, blueberries, yogurt, ice and pineapple juice in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add more ice and blend for a thicker smoothie. Serve immediately.
